## Construct-validity status

This release ships **Φ** and **U** only. Construct validity is asserted at
the *operational* level (do the metrics compute deterministically and
reproducibly?) but **not** at the *interpretive* level (do they measure the
Guattarian concept?). Interpretive validity is gated on v0.2.0 external peers.
