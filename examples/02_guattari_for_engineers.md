# Example 02: Guattari for engineers (reading guide)

This file is a curated reading order for engineers approaching *Schizoanalytic
Cartographies* without a continental-philosophy background. Pair it with
[../docs/guattari_for_engineers.md](../docs/guattari_for_engineers.md) for
the operational mapping.

## Suggested reading order

1. **Pasquinelli, *The Eye of the Master* (2023), ch. 1–2** — economic
   genealogy of "intelligence" that sets up why mechanism-only readings of
   ML undershoot.
2. **Segovia 2025, "Guattari's four-functor chaosmosis"** — concise modern
   summary of the four functors. Free at
   <https://www.academia.edu/128192779/>.
3. **Crowe 2019, "Micropolitics of a Recommender System"** —
   schizoanalytic reading of an actual ML system; useful as concrete
   precedent.
4. **letovacc, *Reading Guattari's Schizoanalytic Cartographies* (substack)**
   — slow read of the original text.
5. **Guattari, *Schizoanalytic Cartographies* (1989; Athlone 2013 trans.)**,
   esp. "The New Aesthetic Paradigm" and "Schizoanalytic Metamodelisation".

## Suggested watching/listening

- Yuk Hui — *Recursivity and Contingency* lectures (computational philosophy
  framing).
- Sha Xin Wei — Topological Media Lab talks (continuous a-signifying media).

## Why we ask you to read at all

The four functors are not interchangeable with circuit-level interpretability
metrics. If you try to translate "Φ ≈ SAE features" without reading at least
items 2 and 3, you will reinvent something already published and miss the
*chaosmotic cycle* that ties the four together.
